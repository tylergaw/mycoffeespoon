Born in Pakistan, raised in NYC and Swedish at heart, the photographer captures her world with analog and digital cameras. With her work, she hopes to bring cohesion between the rice fields of her motherland, NYC pavement and the snow-covered terrain of her adopted home.

She is often inspired by smiling children and men with guitars.

When she isn't shooting, the photographer is making a pot of coconut curry, defending V.S. Naipaul's writing or planning yet another trip. Her goal in life is visit Kamnik, Slovenia.

The photographer's work has appeared on BrooklynVegan, The Swede Beat and Debaser Slussen.
